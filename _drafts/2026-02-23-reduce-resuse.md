# Think about what you have

I have been tying a Fiio EcoSky for about a month.  This was something that I thought I neede dto make sure that I am not around my phone for doom scrolling.  It would be something that I can bring for walks and does not ring every 5 minutes.

I saw a post on TikTok (I know).  This was try re-using an old phone to do this.  I opened my drawer and pulled out my old rooted Moto G5 and started to charge it.  It was great:

 * Physical headphone jack
 * Replaceable battery
 * MicroSD card

So I was playing with LineageOS on the device before so I already had a rooted device.  I installed f-Droid so that I could install apps without having to login to my google account.  I installed

* Odin (Great MusicPlace)
* LocalSend (Transfer Music)

LocalSend worked great for sending music to the phone.  

I will update with the use of it as I keep using it.
